"MY FAVORITES"

FAVORITE COLORS

My ultimate favorite color is white, but I often find myself gravitating towards blue when choosing clothes. Despite blue not being my top favorite, it seems to dominate my wardrobe. I also incorporate colors into my projects and daily items, and if there's an option to choose pink, I'll likely go for it.

FAVORITE FLOWERS

Amongst my favorite flowers, Baby's Breath holds a special allure despite its typical role as a bouquet filler. I find its simplicity captivating, and it adds a delicate charm to arrangements. Tulips, with their varied hues, never fail to captivate me; the way they evoke different emotions while maintaining an inherent elegance is truly remarkable. I'm drawn not only to their visual appeal but also to the deep connection they hold with love. Additionally, Sampaguita, our national flower, has found a place in my heart. Its clean and pristine appearance, coupled with a delightful fragrance, sets it apart. The Sampaguita's unique combination of beauty and a pleasing scent makes it stand out among other flowers, adding a distinct touch to my floral favorites.

FAVORITE FOOD

Sinigang and Bistek Tagalog top my list of favorite dishes. Sinigang, with its comforting tamarind-based broth and hearty mix of meat and vegetables, always warms my soul. Meanwhile, Bistek Tagalog's succulent beef, marinated in a flavorful blend of soy sauce and calamansi, creates a mouthwatering experience with its savory and tangy notes. These two Filipino delights, with their distinct yet complementary flavors, never fail to satisfy my culinary cravings.

FAVORITE DESSERT

Leche flan and choco mousse are my dessert favorites, each providing a unique and delightful experience. The creamy caramelized custard of leche flan and the luscious layers of chocolate goodness in choco mousse never cease to satisfy my sweet cravings with their distinct textures and flavors.
